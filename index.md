## 🚀 How to Create Your First Video

The tool uses a simple workspace. Once you open the program, follow this process to build your first video:

### 1. Prepare your script
Write your explainer content in a basic text file. Use clear headings to separate the sections. This helps the tool understand which parts of your video need graphics versus purely spoken audio. Save this file in a folder where you can find it easily.

### 2. Import your files
Open the project window inside the software. Click the import button to select your text file. The tool scans your script and breaks it down into unique scenes. You will see a list of scenes appearing in the left sidebar.

### 3. Choose your visual style
The software provides several templates for your motion graphics. Choose a layout that fits your topic. You can preview how the graphics look by clicking the play button next to each scene. The tool automatically maps your text to these graphic placeholders.

### 4. Generate the audio
Move to the sound tab. Select a voice profile for your Chinese narration. The tool turns your script into high-quality audio. You can adjust the speed of the speaker based on your preference. Click the voice sync button to match the audio with the text on the screen.

### 5. Review captions and quality
Check the captions generated by the tool. If you need to fix a character or a word, click the text box and edit it directly. Once you finish your edits, click the QA button. The tool reviews the video for timing errors or missing captions. It provides a report if you need to make changes before exporting.

### 6. Export your project
When you feel ready, click the Export button. Choose the 2K resolution setting for the best results. The software processes your video and saves it to your computer. Depending on the length of your script, this step may take a few minutes.
